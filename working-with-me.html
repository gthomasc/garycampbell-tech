Working With Me

I help mission-driven organizations modernize technology, adopt AI responsibly, and strengthen cybersecurity governance — drawing on twenty years of operating experience inside the kinds of organizations I now advise.

The Practice

My advisory work is delivered through Compoundry Advisors — a strategic IT and AI adoption practice for cooperatives, nonprofits, and mid-market organizations whose technology decisions need to align with their mission, not just their margin.

I do this work because I've been the person on the other side of the desk. I've inherited legacy systems, navigated cybersecurity audits with limited resources, rolled out AI tools to teams that were skeptical for good reason, and learned what makes change actually stick in mission-driven cultures.

Most of my engagements start with a conversation. The good ones start with a real problem — not a request for a tool.

Who I Work With

Cooperatives & Member-Owned Organizations

Co-ops face technology decisions with a unique constraint: every decision is also a values decision. I bring the operational experience of working inside one and the technology depth to make the choices defensible to your board, your members, and your team.

Mid-Market Nonprofits

Nonprofits where IT is too important to leave to a generalist but too small to justify a full-time CIO. I provide the strategic layer — vendor governance, AI adoption planning, cybersecurity posture, technology roadmap — without the overhead.

Mission-Driven Mid-Market

For-profit organizations where mission and margin both matter. Hospitality, education, healthcare, and member-services businesses where the people side of technology change is as load-bearing as the systems side.

Boards & Executive Teams

Quarterly or as-needed strategic advisory for boards and executive teams making technology and AI decisions outside their core expertise. Plain-language briefings, vendor evaluations, and risk assessments.

What I Help With

AI Adoption Strategy

Practical, human-in-the-loop AI implementation. Where AI helps, where it doesn't, and how to roll it out without eroding the trust your team has built. Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT for Enterprise, custom AI workflows, and the governance to keep them safe.

Cybersecurity Governance

NIST CSF implementation, vendor security review, staff awareness programs, tabletop exercises, and the cultural work that turns "the IT department's problem" into a shared organizational practice.

Technology Roadmap & Modernization

Multi-year technology strategy that respects what's already working, names what isn't, and sequences change in a way your team can absorb. Cloud migration, SaaS rationalization, infrastructure planning.

Board & Executive Briefings

Plain-language explanations of technology, AI, and cybersecurity topics for non-technical leadership audiences. Board prep, exec onboarding, vendor evaluation, and decision support.

How Engagements Work

Discovery Conversation (free, 30 minutes)

We talk about what's actually going on. No pitch deck. By the end, either there's a clear next step or there isn't — and either answer is useful.

Focused Assessment

A scoped engagement to deeply understand your situation — current state, real constraints, what success looks like. You walk away with a written assessment and a recommended path forward, whether or not you continue with me.

Strategic Project

Defined-scope engagements for specific outcomes — AI adoption strategy, cybersecurity governance program, technology roadmap, vendor selection. Time-boxed, milestone-based, with clear deliverables.

Ongoing Advisory Retainer

Monthly or quarterly cadence for organizations that benefit from a senior strategic IT/AI thinking partner without carrying the full-time cost. Includes board briefings, executive office hours, and on-call escalation for technology decisions.
